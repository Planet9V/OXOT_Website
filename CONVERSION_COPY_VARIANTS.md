## 8. Testing Recommendations

### Test Priority Queue (First 90 Days)

**Week 1-2: Baseline Measurement**
- Deploy control copy across all gates
- Measure: Conversion rate, form completion rate, MQL rate
- Goal: Establish baseline (target: 15-20% conversion)

**Week 3-4: Headline Test #1**
- **Control:** "Unlock Full Article"
- **Variant A:** "[Specific Article Type]: Access Complete [Framework/Analysis/Guide]"
- **Variant B:** "Join 2,000+ OT Professionals Who've Downloaded This Research"
- **Success Metric:** >20% relative uplift in conversion rate
- **Sample Size:** 1,000+ visitors per variant (statistical significance)

**Week 5-6: CTA Button Test #1**
- **Control:** "Continue Reading â†’"
- **Variant A:** "Get Instant Access â†’"
- **Variant B:** "Download Research â†’"
- **Success Metric:** >15% relative uplift in conversion rate

**Week 7-8: Subheadline Test #1**
- **Control:** Generic description
- **Variant A:** Social proof ("Used by 200+ facilities...")
- **Variant B:** Outcome-focused ("Save 40+ hours of research...")
- **Success Metric:** >10% relative uplift in conversion rate

**Week 9-10: Trust Signal Placement**
- **Control:** Trust signals below form
- **Variant A:** Trust signals above form (early reassurance)
- **Variant B:** Trust signals inline with fields (contextual)
- **Success Metric:** Reduce form abandonment by 20%

### Success Criteria

**Headline Test Success:**
- Primary: Conversion rate increase >15%
- Secondary: Lead quality score increase >5%
- Guard rail: Form completion rate decrease <10% (ensure headline doesn't attract wrong audience)

- Primary: Conversion rate increase >10%
