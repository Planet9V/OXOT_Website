## Executive Summary

This document outlines a comprehensive lead capture strategy for OXOT's ArticlesPage, designed to convert anonymous technical content consumers into qualified leads without sacrificing search engine visibility. The strategy employs a three-tier gating approach (Hard Gate, Soft Gate, Ungated) combined with progressive profiling to maximize both conversion rate and lead quality.

**Expected Impact:**
- **Lead Capture Rate:** 15-25% of engaged article readers (industry benchmark: 10-20%)
- **Lead Quality Score:** 70%+ qualified leads (based on role, company size, challenge alignment)
- **SEO Preservation:** 100% (first 200-300 words remain ungated for crawlers)
- **Time to First Lead:** < 24 hours post-implementation

- Email, Company, Role, Company Size, Challenge

- Industry vertical
- Current security maturity level
- Timeline for project/initiative

- Preferred contact method

**Implementation:**
- Use browser localStorage to track returning visitors
- Pre-fill known fields
- Ask only NEW questions on repeat visits

## 3. A/B Testing Roadmap

### 3.1 Priority Test Queue (First 90 Days)

**Week 1-2: Baseline Establishment**
- Control: Current implementation (Hard Gate with 5 fields)
- Metric: Conversion rate, completion rate, drop-off points

**Week 3-4: Form Length Test**
- **Variant A:** 3 fields only (Email, Company, Role)
- **Variant B:** 5 fields (current)

- Includes primary keywords and H1/H2 tags
- Contains article metadata (author, date, category)

```html
<!-- Standard Meta Tags -->
```

## 10. Risk Mitigation & Contingencies

### 10.1 Potential Risks

**Risk: Lower-than-expected conversion rates (<10%)**

*Mitigation:*
- Reduce form fields to email + company only (soft gate all content)
- A/B test social login (LinkedIn SSO)
- Offer PDF download option (lower friction than in-browser unlock)
- Add live chat option: "Have questions? Chat with us to get instant access"

**Risk: High bounce rate (users leave when seeing gate)**

*Mitigation:*
- Increase preview content to 400-500 words (more value before gate)
- Add "Continue reading" anchor link that scrolls to form (vs. appearing as blocker)
- Show # of downloads or social proof ("Join 2,000+ OT security professionals")
- Implement exit-intent popup with simplified form

**Risk: Low lead quality (too many unqualified leads)**

*Mitigation:*
- Add company size minimum threshold (filter out <50 employees)
- Require business email (block free providers more aggressively)
- Add CAPTCHA to prevent bot submissions
- Implement phone number verification for high-value content

**Risk: SEO rankings drop after gating content**

*Mitigation:*
- Increase ungated preview to first 50% of article (vs. 20-30%)
- Keep 10-15 articles completely ungated as SEO drivers
- Ensure preview content includes all primary keywords
- Monitor rankings weekly and adjust gate placement if needed
